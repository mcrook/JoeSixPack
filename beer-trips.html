Christmas Markets & Beer – Only 5 cabins left

A river cruise on the Danube with Joe Sixpack – Dec. 14-21, 2015

Hosted by internationally recognized and award-winning Beer Reporter Joe Sixpack, this once-in-a-lifetime river cruise sets sail for 7 nights from Nuremberg, Germany to Budapest, Hungary, with an optional two-night visit to Prague.

As with all AmaWaterways cruises your excursions are included as well as beer, wine and soft drinks with lunch and dinner. This cruise will offer beer theme excursions in additional to all excursions normally offered on their Christmas Time Cruise. Host Joe Sixpack will be joining you on the excursions as well as offering beer tastings, lectures and other events on board.

This amazing itinerary sails down the beautiful Danube river and crosses Europe’s Continental Divide. Not
only will you be tasting unique beers along the way you will have the opportunity to finish your Christmas shopping at all the wonderful Christmas Markets.
