Drink Beer There - South Jersey Brewery Excursion

Join Joe Sixpack on a daylong luxury coach tour to three of the Garden State's newest beer destinations, with ample samples of fresh ale and lager included.

Each stop includes a fun, informative Q&A "interview" with Joe Sixpack and selected brewers.

Sunday, June 14, 2016

RESERVE YOUR SEAT NOW AND SAVE $10

$59 Early bird (thru June 1)>

Discover the new breed of New Jersey breweries

Forgotten Boardwalk - Cherry Hill

Meet Growler the Cat, play old-fashioned skee-ball and enjoy that Funnel Cake Boardwalk Ale you've been hearing about.

 

 

Village Idiot - Mount Holly

One of Jersey's smallest breweries is producing unique small-batch varieties, including Teddy Hopper (not to be confused with you know what). Grab a crowler to go!

 

 

Spellbound - Mount Holly

You may have seen their cans, but have you tried their taproom-only favorites, yet? Peach 2X IPA, Oatmeal Raisin Cookie and more.
